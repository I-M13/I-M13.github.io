Job search and

Career Opporunity

Platforms

1. Bright Network

Bright Network is a UK-based careers platform connecting students and graduates with top employers through job opportunities, career advice, and networking events.

2. Target Jobs

A UK job board offering graduate, internship, and apprenticeship opportunities, along with career advice and employer insights for students and recent graduates.

3. Huzzle

A platform connecting students and graduates with top employers through virtual internships, work experience, and career events to enhance professional skills and job prospects.

4. Employment4Students

A job board focused on part-time work, internships, and graduate roles, specifically tailored for students and recent graduates looking to balance work and study.

Diversity and

Inclusivity

Career Support

1. SEO London

A UK-based organization providing training, mentorship, and career support for underrepresented groups in digital marketing and technology, aiming to improve diversity in the industry.

2. WCAN

A platform dedicated to supporting women in their careers through networking opportunities, career development resources, and mentoring programs across various industries.

3. 10K Black Interns

A UK initiative aiming to create 10,000 paid internship opportunities for Black students and graduates to enhance career prospects and promote diversity in the workplace.

4. Upreach

A career development organization offering tailored support, including mentoring and application coaching, to help underrepresented students and graduates secure internships and graduate roles.
